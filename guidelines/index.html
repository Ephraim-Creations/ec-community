Community Guidelines

Creating a Safe, Respectful, and Valuable Environment for Everyone

Age Restriction: 18+ Only

Our communities are exclusively for individuals aged 18 years and above. By participating, you confirm you meet this requirement.

Our Community Values

EC Community is built on mutual respect, collaboration, and professional growth. These guidelines ensure everyone has a positive experience.

Be Respectful

Treat all members with kindness and professionalism

Learn Together

Share knowledge and help others grow

Collaborate

Work together on projects and ideas

Stay Safe

Protect your privacy and others'

Zero Tolerance - Immediate Removal

These violations result in immediate and permanent removal from all EC Community groups.

No Harassment or Discrimination

Absolutely no hate speech, bullying, racism, sexism, or any form of discrimination against any individual or group.

Consequence IMMEDIATE BAN

Permanent removal from all EC Community groups without warning.

No Illegal Content

Sharing or promoting illegal activities, pirated content, or harmful materials is strictly prohibited.

Consequence IMMEDIATE BAN

Permanent removal and reporting to authorities if necessary.

Major Rules - Warnings Apply

These violations typically result in warnings before removal.

No Unsolicited Marketing

Do not spam the group with promotional content, affiliate links, or unsolicited business offers without admin permission.

Consequence 1 WARNING THEN REMOVAL

First offense: Warning and message deletion. Second offense: Removal from group.

Respect Privacy

Do not share others' personal information, contact details, or private conversations without explicit consent.

Consequence IMMEDIATE REMOVAL

Privacy violations result in immediate removal due to their serious nature.

No Drama or Arguments

Keep discussions professional. Avoid heated arguments, personal attacks, or creating unnecessary drama.

Consequence 2 WARNINGS THEN REMOVAL

First offense: Warning. Second: Final warning. Third: Removal from group.

Community Expectations

These guidelines help maintain the quality of our community discussions.

Use Relevant Channels

Post content in appropriate groups. Devs Hub for technical discussions, Insight Room for strategy and marketing topics.

Be Helpful & Constructive

Provide helpful feedback, answer questions when you can, and contribute positively to discussions.

English Language Only

To ensure everyone can participate and understand, please use English in all group communications.

Reporting Issues

Help us maintain a safe community by reporting guideline violations.

Contact Admins

Message any admin directly with concerns

Provide Evidence

Screenshot violations when reporting

Respect Decisions

Trust admin judgments on rule enforcement

Follow All Guidelines

These guidelines are enforced to ensure EC Community remains a valuable, safe, and professional space for all members.

Ready to Join?
