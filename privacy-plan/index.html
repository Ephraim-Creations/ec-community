Privacy Policy

Transparent. Simple. Respectful of Your Privacy.

Our Privacy Commitment

"This website doesn't collect any personal information. We simply share links to join our WhatsApp communities."

Age Restriction

Our communities are for individuals aged 18 years and above. By joining our WhatsApp groups, you confirm that you meet this age requirement.

Complete Privacy Policy

Information Collection

We want to be completely transparent about what information we do and don't collect:

  • We DO NOT collect any personal information on this website
  • We DO NOT use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics
  • We DO NOT store any data about our visitors
  • We DO NOT require registrations, sign-ups, or forms

    • Third-Party Services

    When you click on WhatsApp join links, you'll be directed to WhatsApp's platform. Please review WhatsApp's privacy policy for information on how they handle your data.

    Community Guidelines

    While we don't collect data on this website, our WhatsApp communities have clear guidelines:

  • Respect all community members
  • No spam or promotional content without permission
  • Maintain professional and constructive discussions
  • Protect the privacy of other members
  • Adhere to WhatsApp's terms of service
  • more on our guidelines

    • Your Rights

    Since we don't collect any personal information, you have complete privacy when browsing our website. Your visit is untracked.

    Policy Updates

    Should our practices change in the future, we will update this policy and notify our community members. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and trust.

    Contact

    If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our WhatsApp communities.
